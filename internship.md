# Internship Opportunity for Undergraduate Student Volunteers

We are looking for undergraduate volunteers to assist during the sessions of the SDSC Summer Institute 2026!

## Session Details
- **Session Dates:** Summer Institute dates will be announced soon.
- We are seeking a different student for each session.

## Volunteer Responsibilities
- Review the session content in advance.
- Be present in the room during the session.
- Walk around and help attendees during hands-on tutorials if they get stuck.

## Internship Credit
Volunteers will receive internship credit for their hours.

## Requirements
Students should be familiar with the Linux command line and git commands in the terminal.
