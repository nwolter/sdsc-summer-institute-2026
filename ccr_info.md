# Co-Curricular Record (CCR) for Summer Institute Interns

## Overview

Student volunteers who assist during the Summer Institute sessions can earn **Co-Curricular Record (CCR)** credit. CCR is an official UC San Diego record printed with the student's transcript that recognizes experiences and skills developed beyond the classroom. Students can use their CCR transcript to help write cover letters, resumes, or prepare for interviews.

## Key Requirements

### For Students to Earn CCR Credit

- **30 hours minimum** of active engagement is required for CCR recognition
- CIML will not use interns this year, so all hours must come from the **SDSC Summer Institute 2026**
- SDSC Summer Institute dates: July 23 – August 7, 2026
- The plan is to assign a **different student helper to each session**. A single session alone is too short, so each student's 30 hours will be built from:
  - Pre-session preparation: review content, test exercises, meet instructor (~6–10 hours)
  - Assigned session attendance and hands-on support (~2–4 hours)
  - Follow-up: clarify exercises, answer questions, debrief (~2–4 hours)
  - General institute support: orientation prep, logistics, setup/breakdown across additional half-days (~10–15 hours)
- Students must be enrolled at UC San Diego during the academic year
- The coordinator (faculty/staff validator) must verify participation

### Supervisor / Coordinator Responsibilities

1. **Register as a CCR Validator** (if not already):
   - Log into the CCR Portal: https://realportal.ucsd.edu (Faculty & Staff SSO)
   - Email `elt@ucsd.edu` to be designated as validator for the approved position

2. **Track Participation Hours**:
   - Use the CCR Participation Tracker template or equivalent
   - Ensure students accumulate at least 30 hours of verified engagement
   - Inform interns of the validation timeline and tracking expectations at the start of the opportunity

3. **Complete Validation**:
   - Approve validation requests after the 30-hour requirement is met
   - Validation deadline for the 2025–2026 academic year: **June 30, 2026** (SOFT deadline)
   - Validation can be initiated by the student or managed independently by the validator
   - Validation can be done after the deadline; programs with summer participation hours are expected to validate in late summer

## Student Instructions for Adding CCR

1. Log into the CCR with student SSO credentials at: **https://realportal.ucsd.edu/ccr.htm**
2. Click the **"Co-Curricular Module"** link in the left navigation menu
3. Click the **"Add an Experience"** button underneath your name
4. Ensure the correct academic year is selected under the **"Period"** dropdown
5. Search for **"SDSC Summer Institute"** in the right keyword search bar
6. Click the **"+"** button on the correct position (will show "Add to Record" when hovering)
7. Once added, the identified UCSD staff/faculty member will confirm participation by approving the request
8. Until validated, the position will appear as **"Pending"** in your Co-Curricular Module page
