# SDSC Summer Institute 2026 -- Student Helper Onboarding

Welcome! Thank you for volunteering as a student helper for the SDSC Summer Institute 2026.

**Dates:** July 23 -- August 7, 2026
**Full agenda:** [AGENDA.md](./AGENDA.md)

## What You'll Do

- Review your assigned session content in advance.
- Be present in the room during your sessions.
- Walk around and help attendees during hands-on tutorials if they get stuck.

Each helper is assigned **2 sessions** as primary support.

Sessions without helpers: Overview of SDSC Supercomputers, Closing Remarks, Data Center Tour, Getting Help.

## Your Two Paths

All helpers have the same core duties. The only difference is whether you are seeking **CCR credit** or not.

### Path A: Seeking CCR Credit

The Co-Curricular Record (CCR) is an official UC San Diego record printed with your transcript that recognizes experiences and skills developed beyond the classroom.

**Requirement:** 30 hours minimum of active engagement.

**Your hours breakdown:**
- Prep Day (July 23, virtual): ~3 hours
- ACCESS account setup: ~2-3 hours
- Pre-session preparation for your 2 sessions (review content, test exercises, meet instructors): ~12-20 hours
- Assigned session attendance and hands-on support: ~3-7 hours
- Follow-up (clarify exercises, answer attendee questions, debrief with instructor): ~2-4 hours
- **Attend 1 additional session as a regular participant:** ~1.5-3.5 hours
- **Total: ~23.5-40.5 hours** (above the 30-hour minimum)

**How to add CCR to your record:**
1. Log into the CCR Portal with your student SSO: [https://realportal.ucsd.edu/ccr.htm](https://realportal.ucsd.edu/ccr.htm)
2. Click **"Co-Curricular Module"** in the left navigation
3. Click **"Add an Experience"** under your name
4. Select the correct academic year under **"Period"**
5. Search for **"Summer Education Program Assistant"** (this is the exact position title under "SDSC Education and Outreach")
6. Click the **"+"** on the matching position
7. Your participation will appear as **"Pending"** until validated by your coordinator

**Note:** Searching for "SDSC Summer Institute" may not return results -- use the exact position name above.

Need help? Contact the [Experiential Learning Hub](https://experientiallearning.ucsd.edu/ccr/index.html).

### Path B: Not Seeking CCR Credit

Your duties are the same, minus the extra attendee session.

**Your hours breakdown:**
- Prep Day (July 23, virtual): ~3 hours
- ACCESS account setup: ~2-3 hours
- Pre-session preparation for your 2 sessions: ~12-20 hours
- Assigned session attendance and hands-on support: ~3-7 hours
- Follow-up: ~2-4 hours
- **Total: ~22-37 hours**

No CCR registration step needed.
